## 2024-04-26 - Accessible Accordion Headers
**Learning:** In the codebase, accordion headers are often implemented as clickable `div` elements with `onClick` handlers and `cursor-pointer`. While these work for mouse users, they lack necessary ARIA attributes, semantic roles, and keyboard navigation support, making them inaccessible to screen readers and keyboard-only users.
**Action:** When creating custom clickable elements (like accordion headers) using `div`s, ensure they receive `role="button"`, `tabIndex={0}`, `aria-expanded` (if toggleable), an `onKeyDown` handler to process 'Enter' and 'Space' keys, and `focus-visible` utility classes for visual focus indicators.
