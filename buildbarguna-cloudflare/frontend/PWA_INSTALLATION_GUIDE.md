# iOS PWA Installation Guide

## How to Install বিল্ড বরগুনা on iPhone/iPad

Follow these steps to install the বিল্ড বরগুনা app on your iOS device for a native app-like experience.

### Step 1: Open in Safari

1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone or iPad
2. Navigate to the app URL (e.g., `https://buildbarguna.com`)

> **Important:** You must use Safari. The "Add to Home Screen" feature is not available in Chrome, Firefox, or other browsers on iOS.

### Step 2: Tap the Share Button

1. Tap the **Share** icon at the bottom of the screen (square with arrow pointing up)
   - On iPhone: Bottom center of screen
   - On iPad: Top right of screen

### Step 3: Add to Home Screen

1. Scroll down in the share menu
2. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. You'll see a preview of the app icon and name

### Step 4: Confirm Installation

1. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner
2. The app icon will appear on your home screen

### Step 5: Launch the App

1. Find the বিল্ড বরগুনা icon on your home screen
2. Tap to launch the app
3. The app will open in fullscreen mode (no Safari browser UI)

## Features

Once installed, the app provides:

- **Fullscreen Experience**: No browser address bar or navigation
- **Offline Access**: App loads even without internet (cached content)
- **Fast Loading**: Instant load from cache on subsequent visits
- **Auto Updates**: App updates automatically in the background
- **Home Screen Icon**: Easy access like a native app

## Troubleshooting

### App doesn't open in fullscreen

Make sure you added it to the home screen using Safari. Apps opened directly from Safari bookmarks won't run in fullscreen mode.

### Content is outdated

1. Pull down from the top of the app to refresh
2. Or close and reopen the app

### App shows "No internet connection"

The app needs an initial internet connection to load. After first load, it works offline with cached content.

### Icon doesn't look right

The app uses the official বিল্ড বরগুনা logo. If you see a different icon, try:
1. Remove the app from home screen (long press → Remove App)
2. Clear Safari cache (Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data)
3. Re-add the app to home screen

## Removing the App

To remove the app from your home screen:

1. Long press the app icon
2. Tap **"Remove App"**
3. Tap **"Delete"** to confirm

This only removes the shortcut—your account data remains safe on our servers.

## System Requirements

- **iOS Version**: iOS 15.0 or later (recommended)
- **Browser**: Safari only
- **Storage**: ~5MB for app cache
- **Internet**: Required for initial setup, optional for offline use

## Support

If you experience any issues:
- Check your iOS version (Settings → General → About)
- Ensure you're using Safari
- Try clearing Safari cache and re-adding the app
- Contact support for assistance

---

**Note for Android Users:** If you're on Android, you can install the native Capacitor app or use the PWA following similar steps (Chrome → Menu → "Add to Home screen").
